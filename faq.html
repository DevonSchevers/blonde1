FAQ

What are the earrings made of?

All Pieces of Blonde earrings are made of 100% polymer clay (aside from any brass findings or accents.)

Are patterns painted onto the earrings?

All patterns are handcrafted using clay. No earring is painted. This means that no two earring patterns will be exactly alike, making it special for each owner :)

What types of earring posts are used? I have allergies and/or sensitive skin.

Each earring is set or hung on 18K gold plated, hypo-allergenic surgical steel hooks or studs. They are lead and nickel free, and should not cause irritation.

How heavy are the earrings?

The best part of Pieces of Blonde is that each earring is ULTRA lightweight! I constantly have customers send me private messages commenting on how surprised they are by how lightweight my earrings are. YES, even the large statement ones :)

Does Pieces of Blonde ship internationally?

Right now, we only ship within the U.S. (including Hawaii & Alaska).

How are the earrings packaged?

Each earring is shipped inside a small box with padding and mailed in a poly-mailer.
